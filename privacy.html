Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Get Blink Facts ("we," "our") publishes short factual and educational videos across YouTube, TikTok, and other platforms. This policy explains what this application does and what data it touches when it publishes our videos to TikTok.

In short: this application publishes our own videos to our own TikTok account. It does not collect, view, or store any data belonging to TikTok viewers, other creators, or the general public.

What this application is

This is a publishing tool operated solely by Get Blink Facts. Its only function is to upload video content — produced by us — to the @GetBlinkFacts TikTok account, using TikTok's official Content Posting API. It is not a public-facing product, and it has no separate user accounts.

Data we access

To publish videos on our behalf, this application accesses:

  • Authentication tokens for the @GetBlinkFacts TikTok account, issued through TikTok's own OAuth login flow.
  • Basic account identifiers (such as the account's open ID) needed to address API requests to the correct account.

We do not access, request, or store data belonging to any other TikTok account, and we do not collect any information about people who watch, like, comment on, or share our videos.

Data we store

Authentication tokens are stored securely and used only to authorize publishing requests to TikTok's API. Video files and captions are the content we create ourselves for our own channel. We do not maintain any database of TikTok users or viewer information.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Video content is shared publicly on TikTok as ordinary published posts, exactly as if we had uploaded them through the TikTok app.

Your rights

If you are a TikTok user with questions about how TikTok itself handles your data, please refer to TikTok's own Privacy Policy. For questions about this application specifically, see the contact information below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as this application's use of TikTok's API changes. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.

Contact: reach Get Blink Facts through the Get Blink Facts YouTube channel.
